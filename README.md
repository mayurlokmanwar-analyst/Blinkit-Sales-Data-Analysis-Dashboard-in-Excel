## 🚀 Strategic Overview
This project transforms **$1.2M in raw grocery transaction data** into a sophisticated Sales & Inventory Intelligence suite. Built entirely within **Microsoft Excel**, this dashboard evaluates outlet efficiency, item performance, and consumer health trends to provide actionable insights for the hyper-local delivery model.

### 📈 Core Business KPIs
| **Metric** | **Value** | **Business Impact** |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Total Revenue** | **$1.20M** | Aggregate sales across all outlet tiers. |
| **Avg Transaction** | **$141** | High-value customer basket identification. |
| **Sales Volume** | **8,523** | Total inventory units tracked and optimized. |
| **Health Focus** | **$776K** | Low-fat items outperforming regular stock. |

## 💡 Strategic Business Insights
* **Regional Dominance:** **Tier 3 locations** emerged as the primary revenue drivers, proving that hyper-local demand is highest in emerging markets.
* **Department Winners:** **Fruits & Vegetables ($178K)** and **Snack Foods ($175K)** identified as the critical categories for inventory stocking.
* **Growth Correlation:** Mapped establishment dates (2011–2022) to prove a direct link between physical outlet expansion and revenue scaling.
* **Customer Sentiment:** Maintained a network-wide average rating of **3.9/5**, setting the benchmark for service quality.
